# 04_evidence_engine.md

# Enterprise Business Transformation Consulting Framework

## Evidence & Confidence Engine

**Version:** 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

The Evidence Engine governs how facts are collected, validated,
weighted, reconciled, and propagated throughout the consulting
framework.

No recommendation SHALL exist without traceable evidence.

Every conclusion SHALL be explainable back to one or more supporting
artifacts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Guiding Principles

-   Evidence SHALL always outweigh assumptions.
-   Missing evidence SHALL reduce confidence.
-   Contradictions SHALL be surfaced, not ignored.
-   The framework SHALL distinguish facts from interpretations.
-   Every recommendation SHALL maintain an evidence trail.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Evidence Categories

Classify every observation as one of:

1.  Direct Observation
2.  System Evidence
3.  Process Demonstration
4.  Meeting Transcript
5.  SOP / Documentation
6.  Data Export
7.  Interview Statement
8.  Screenshot / Recording
9.  Inferred Relationship
10. Assumption

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Source Reliability

Assign a reliability score:

  Source                                Reliability
  ----------------------------------- -------------
  Live workflow observation                     100
  System logs / reports                          95
  Screen recordings                              90
  Multiple stakeholder confirmation              90
  Official SOP                                   80
  Individual interview                           70
  Manager opinion                                60
  Founder assumption                             55
  Consultant inference                           40

Reliability SHALL be adjusted when evidence conflicts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Evidence Object Schema

Each evidence item SHOULD contain:

-   Evidence ID
-   Source
-   Artifact Reference
-   Date (if known)
-   Business Area
-   Summary
-   Reliability Score
-   Confidence Contribution
-   Related Entities
-   Related Workflow
-   Related Recommendation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Confidence Calculation

Confidence SHALL consider:

-   Source reliability
-   Number of independent confirmations
-   Artifact coverage
-   Contradictions
-   Completeness of process understanding

Suggested interpretation:

-   95--100: Confirmed
-   85--94: High Confidence
-   70--84: Moderate Confidence
-   50--69: Low Confidence
-   Below 50: Insufficient Evidence

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Contradiction Engine

When two or more sources disagree:

Record:

-   Conflicting Statements
-   Source A
-   Source B
-   Possible Explanations
-   Business Impact
-   Clarification Required

Do NOT silently choose a preferred answer unless objective evidence
exists.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Evidence Traceability

Every recommendation SHALL reference:

-   Supporting Evidence IDs
-   Supporting Business Graph Nodes
-   Related Workflow
-   Related Root Cause
-   Confidence Score

Recommendations lacking traceability SHALL fail quality validation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Assumption Registry

Maintain a registry of assumptions.

Each assumption SHALL include:

-   Description
-   Reason for Assumption
-   Confidence
-   Required Validation
-   Potential Impact if Incorrect

Assumptions SHALL NEVER be presented as facts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Missing Evidence Detection

Actively identify:

-   Missing stakeholder interviews
-   Missing workflow steps
-   Missing approvals
-   Missing KPIs
-   Missing reports
-   Missing compliance information
-   Missing customer touchpoints

Generate clarification requests where appropriate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Confidence Propagation

Confidence SHALL propagate through dependent findings.

Example:

If onboarding workflow confidence is low, then downstream automation
recommendations SHALL also reduce confidence.

The framework SHALL avoid overstating certainty.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommendation Evidence Matrix

Every recommendation SHALL contain:

-   Observation
-   Supporting Evidence
-   Root Cause
-   Confidence
-   Risk if Wrong
-   Validation Status

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Evidence Quality Gates

Before passing results downstream, verify:

-   Evidence exists for every major finding
-   Contradictions documented
-   Assumptions separated
-   Confidence assigned
-   Missing evidence identified
-   Traceability maintained

Failure of any gate SHALL invoke the Clarification Protocol.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Output Contract

This module SHALL produce:

-   Evidence Register
-   Confidence Matrix
-   Contradiction Register
-   Assumption Register
-   Missing Evidence Report
-   Recommendation Traceability Map

Outputs SHALL be consumed by:

-   Scoring Engine
-   Business Analysis
-   Workflow Analysis
-   Technology Audit
-   Roadmap Generator
-   HTML Report Generator

The Evidence Engine SHALL remain the authoritative source for confidence
and factual integrity across the consulting framework.
