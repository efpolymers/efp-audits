# 05_scoring_engine.md

# Enterprise Business Transformation Consulting Framework

## Scoring & Maturity Engine

**Version:** 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

The Scoring Engine converts qualitative observations into objective,
repeatable business assessments.

Scores SHALL support decision-making, prioritization, benchmarking, and
progress tracking.

Scores SHALL NEVER be assigned without supporting evidence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principles

-   Every score MUST be evidence-backed.
-   Every score MUST be explainable.
-   Low confidence SHALL reduce score confidence.
-   Scores are indicators, not absolute truths.
-   Reassess scores whenever significant new evidence is discovered.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Maturity Scale

Use the following maturity model unless overridden by a domain-specific
module.

  ------------------------------------------------------------------------
                Level Name             Description
  ------------------- ---------------- -----------------------------------
                    1 Initial          Reactive, undocumented, highly
                                       dependent on individuals

                    2 Developing       Basic repeatability with
                                       inconsistent execution

                    3 Standardized     Defined processes adopted across
                                       most teams

                    4 Managed          Measured, governed, and
                                       continuously monitored

                    5 Optimized        Continuously improved, automated,
                                       scalable, data-driven
  ------------------------------------------------------------------------

-   SLA Adherence

-   Reporting Capability

# AI Readiness Index

Evaluate:

-   Data Availability
-   Data Quality
-   Repeatable Processes
-   Decision Standardization
-   Human Oversight
-   Governance
-   Expected ROI

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Automation Readiness

For each workflow determine:

-   Manual Effort
-   Frequency
-   Rule-Based Nature
-   Error Rate
-   Business Impact
-   Automation Complexity

Classify:

-   Automate Immediately
-   Automate After Standardization
-   Monitor
-   Keep Manual

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Knowledge Risk Score

Measure:

-   Tribal Knowledge
-   Documentation Gaps
-   Single-Person Dependencies
-   Onboarding Complexity
-   Knowledge Discoverability

Higher risk SHALL increase transformation priority.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Scalability Score

Simulate:

-   2x Growth
-   5x Growth
-   10x Growth

Evaluate:

-   Process Capacity
-   Team Capacity
-   Technology Capacity
-   Governance Capacity
-   Compliance Capacity

Highlight projected failure points.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Prioritization Matrix

Every recommendation SHALL receive:

-   Business Value (1--10)
-   Urgency (1--10)
-   Effort (1--10)
-   Risk Reduction (1--10)
-   Strategic Alignment (1--10)
-   Customer Impact (1--10)

Generate an overall priority score and recommended implementation phase.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Transformation Readiness

Assess whether the organization is prepared for transformation by
evaluating:

-   Leadership Commitment
-   Resource Availability
-   Budget Readiness
-   Cultural Readiness
-   Technology Readiness
-   Process Maturity

Classify:

-   Not Ready
-   Limited Readiness
-   Ready with Constraints
-   Ready
-   Highly Ready

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Benchmarking Guidance

Where sufficient evidence exists, compare current maturity against:

-   Industry Best Practices
-   Similar Company Size
-   Service-Based Organizations
-   Compliance-Driven Organizations

Clearly state when benchmarks are heuristic rather than empirical.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Score Validation Rules

Reject or flag scores when:

-   Evidence is insufficient.
-   Confidence is below acceptable threshold.
-   Major stakeholders are missing.
-   Core workflows remain unmapped.

Invoke Clarification Protocol before finalizing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Output Contract

This module SHALL produce:

-   Executive Scorecard
-   Department Scorecards
-   Maturity Heatmap
-   Priority Matrix
-   Transformation Readiness Summary
-   Risk Ranking
-   Improvement Backlog

These outputs SHALL feed:

-   Executive Summary
-   HTML Report Generator
-   Roadmap Generator
-   Dashboard Visualizations

The Scoring Engine SHALL provide the quantitative foundation for all
strategic recommendations.
