# Enterprise Business Transformation Consulting Framework

## Consulting Methodology

**Version:** 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

This document defines the end-to-end consulting methodology used
throughout the framework.

The methodology SHALL provide a repeatable approach for understanding a
business, identifying root causes, designing future-state operations,
and delivering an implementable transformation roadmap.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Consulting Lifecycle

Every engagement SHALL follow these phases:

1.  Engagement Preparation
2.  Artifact Collection
3.  Stakeholder Discovery
4.  Current State Assessment
5.  Business Graph Construction
6.  Root Cause Analysis
7.  Opportunity Identification
8.  Future State Design
9.  Prioritization
10. Roadmap Development
11. Executive Review
12. Final Delivery

Progression SHALL occur only when required quality gates are satisfied.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 1 -- Engagement Preparation

Objectives:

-   Define scope
-   Identify stakeholders
-   Confirm objectives
-   Understand constraints
-   Identify success criteria

Deliverables:

-   Scope Statement
-   Stakeholder Map
-   Engagement Objectives
-   Initial Risks

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 2 -- Artifact Collection

Gather all available evidence including:

-   Meeting transcripts
-   SOPs
-   Process documents
-   Policies
-   Organization charts
-   Screenshots
-   CRM exports
-   Reports
-   Dashboards
-   Templates
-   Communication samples

Assess completeness before analysis begins.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 3 -- Stakeholder Discovery

Interview key stakeholders.

Capture:

-   Responsibilities
-   Decisions
-   Pain points
-   Dependencies
-   KPIs
-   Exceptions
-   Technology usage

Validate against collected artifacts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 4 -- Current State Assessment

Develop a comprehensive understanding of:

-   Business model
-   Customer lifecycle
-   Department responsibilities
-   Workflow architecture
-   Technology landscape
-   Data movement
-   Governance
-   Compliance

Document both official and actual processes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 5 -- Root Cause Analysis

Investigate observed issues using:

-   Five Whys
-   Fishbone Analysis
-   Theory of Constraints
-   Systems Thinking
-   Dependency Analysis

Do not recommend solutions before confirming root causes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 6 -- Opportunity Identification

Identify opportunities across:

-   Process
-   Technology
-   Data
-   Automation
-   AI
-   Reporting
-   Governance
-   Customer Experience
-   Compliance

Each opportunity SHALL include expected business value and
implementation complexity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 7 -- Future State Design

Design an operating model covering:

-   Organization
-   Processes
-   Systems
-   Data
-   Governance
-   Automation
-   AI
-   Reporting
-   KPIs

Future-state recommendations SHALL remain realistic for the
organization's maturity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 8 -- Prioritization

Evaluate initiatives using:

-   Business Value
-   Risk Reduction
-   Cost
-   Effort
-   Strategic Alignment
-   Customer Impact
-   Operational Impact

Group into:

-   Quick Wins
-   Short Term
-   Medium Term
-   Long Term

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 9 -- Roadmap Development

Produce a phased implementation plan.

Each initiative SHALL specify:

-   Objective
-   Owner
-   Dependencies
-   Estimated Timeline
-   Required Resources
-   Risks
-   Success Metrics

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 10 -- Executive Review

Validate that:

-   Findings are evidence-backed
-   Risks are documented
-   Recommendations are prioritized
-   Roadmap is feasible
-   Business objectives are addressed

Resolve outstanding questions before final delivery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Methodology Quality Gates

Before finalizing the engagement confirm:

-   Scope complete
-   Evidence sufficient
-   Interviews complete
-   Major workflows mapped
-   Root causes validated
-   Future state defined
-   Recommendations prioritized
-   Risks documented
-   Confidence acceptable

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Deliverables

The methodology SHALL produce:

-   Current State Assessment
-   Business Graph
-   Technology Assessment
-   Workflow Assessment
-   Risk Register
-   Opportunity Backlog
-   Future State Blueprint
-   Transformation Roadmap
-   Executive Summary

These deliverables SHALL serve as inputs to the HTML Report Generator
and executive presentation.
