# 08_business_analysis.md

# Enterprise Business Transformation Consulting Framework

## Business Analysis Engine

**Version:** 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

The Business Analysis Engine develops a complete understanding of how
the organization creates value.

It SHALL identify the current business model, organizational
capabilities, customer lifecycle, operating model, strategic objectives,
constraints, and business risks before any process or technology
recommendations are made.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Objectives

The engine SHALL answer:

-   What business does the organization operate?
-   How does it generate revenue?
-   What differentiates it?
-   What are its strategic objectives?
-   What operational capabilities enable success?
-   What constraints limit growth?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Analysis Scope

Evaluate the following domains:

-   Business Model
-   Revenue Streams
-   Customer Segments
-   Service Portfolio
-   Organizational Structure
-   Governance
-   Value Proposition
-   Delivery Model
-   Competitive Position
-   Strategic Objectives
-   Growth Constraints
-   Business Risks

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Model Assessment

Document:

-   Products / Services
-   Pricing Model
-   Revenue Sources
-   Cost Drivers
-   Recurring vs One-time Revenue
-   Customer Acquisition Channels
-   Customer Retention Strategy
-   Partnerships
-   Geographic Coverage

Highlight dependencies that materially influence profitability.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Customer Analysis

Identify:

-   Customer Personas
-   Customer Segments
-   Customer Journey
-   Buying Process
-   Decision Makers
-   Pain Points
-   Expectations
-   Renewal Behaviour
-   Upsell Opportunities

Map customer interactions across the entire lifecycle.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Organizational Analysis

Capture:

-   Departments
-   Roles
-   Reporting Structure
-   Decision Authority
-   Ownership Boundaries
-   Internal Service Relationships
-   Cross-functional Dependencies

Flag unclear ownership and duplicated responsibilities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Capability Assessment

Evaluate capabilities including:

-   Sales
-   Marketing
-   Delivery
-   Operations
-   Finance
-   HR
-   Compliance
-   Customer Success
-   Technology
-   Data Management

For each capability identify:

-   Current Maturity
-   Strengths
-   Weaknesses
-   Risks
-   Improvement Opportunities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Strategic Alignment

Assess whether:

-   Daily operations support strategic goals.
-   KPIs align with objectives.
-   Teams understand priorities.
-   Investments support long-term growth.

Highlight misalignment between strategy and execution.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Constraint Analysis

Identify constraints across:

-   People
-   Process
-   Technology
-   Data
-   Finance
-   Compliance
-   Leadership
-   Market

Estimate business impact if constraints remain unresolved.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Governance Review

Evaluate:

-   Decision-making
-   Accountability
-   Delegation
-   Performance Reviews
-   Policy Adherence
-   Risk Ownership
-   Escalation Structure

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Risk Register

Classify risks by:

-   Operational
-   Financial
-   Compliance
-   Technology
-   Customer
-   Knowledge
-   Vendor
-   Scalability

For each risk include:

-   Description
-   Likelihood
-   Impact
-   Existing Controls
-   Recommended Mitigation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Opportunity Register

Identify opportunities in:

-   Revenue Growth
-   Cost Reduction
-   Customer Experience
-   Operational Efficiency
-   Automation
-   AI
-   Reporting
-   Governance
-   Knowledge Management

Prioritize using evidence and expected business value.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Health Summary

Produce an executive summary including:

-   Business Strengths
-   Critical Weaknesses
-   Strategic Risks
-   Immediate Priorities
-   Long-term Opportunities
-   Executive Observations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Quality Gates

Before completion verify:

-   Business model understood
-   Customer lifecycle mapped
-   Organizational structure validated
-   Core capabilities assessed
-   Strategic objectives identified
-   Constraints documented
-   Risks prioritized
-   Evidence linked
-   Confidence assigned

Invoke the Clarification Protocol if major uncertainty remains.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Output Contract

This module SHALL produce:

-   Business Model Canvas (structured)
-   Organizational Assessment
-   Capability Assessment
-   Customer Lifecycle Summary
-   Strategic Gap Analysis
-   Business Risk Register
-   Opportunity Register
-   Executive Business Health Report

Outputs SHALL be consumed by:

-   Workflow Analysis
-   Technology Audit
-   Data Flow Analysis
-   AI Transformation
-   Roadmap Generator
-   HTML Report Generator

The Business Analysis Engine SHALL establish the business context for
every downstream recommendation.
