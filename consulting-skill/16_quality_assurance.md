# 16_quality_assurance.md

# Enterprise Business Transformation Consulting Framework

## Quality Assurance & Validation Engine

**Version:** 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

The Quality Assurance Engine validates the integrity, consistency,
completeness, and reliability of every consulting engagement before
client delivery.

Its objective is to ensure that every finding, recommendation, score,
roadmap, and visualization is evidence-backed, internally consistent,
actionable, and implementation-ready.

No deliverable SHALL be finalized until all mandatory quality gates have
been satisfied.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Quality Principles

-   Accuracy before speed.
-   Evidence before opinion.
-   Consistency before completeness.
-   Business value before documentation.
-   Recommendations SHALL be implementable.
-   Uncertainty SHALL be disclosed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Validation Layers

Perform validation across:

1.  Business Understanding
2.  Evidence Integrity
3.  Workflow Completeness
4.  Technology Assessment
5.  Data Assessment
6.  AI Recommendations
7.  Roadmap Feasibility
8.  Executive Readability
9.  Report Quality

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Completeness Checklist

Verify that the engagement contains:

-   Business model
-   Customer lifecycle
-   Organizational structure
-   Department responsibilities
-   Core workflows
-   Technology inventory
-   Data flows
-   Risks
-   KPIs
-   Future-state design
-   Roadmap
-   Executive summary

Flag missing deliverables before completion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Consistency Validation

Cross-check:

-   Recommendations align with findings.
-   Findings align with evidence.
-   Scores align with observations.
-   Roadmap aligns with priorities.
-   AI recommendations align with readiness.
-   Technology recommendations align with business goals.

Document any inconsistencies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommendation Audit

Every recommendation SHALL include:

-   Business Problem
-   Root Cause
-   Evidence
-   Business Value
-   Risks
-   Dependencies
-   Owner
-   Timeline
-   Success Metrics

Reject incomplete recommendations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Confidence Audit

Ensure:

-   Confidence assigned to major findings.
-   Low-confidence findings clearly identified.
-   Assumptions documented.
-   Clarification requests generated where required.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Hallucination Prevention

Reject conclusions that:

-   Lack evidence.
-   Invent workflows.
-   Assume missing approvals.
-   Recommend software without rationale.
-   Present assumptions as facts.

Require clarification when confidence is insufficient.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Review

Assess whether the final report answers:

-   What is happening?
-   Why is it happening?
-   What should change?
-   Why should it change?
-   What value will it create?
-   How should it be implemented?
-   What happens if nothing changes?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Implementation Readiness

Validate:

-   Owners assigned
-   Dependencies identified
-   Timeline realistic
-   Risks managed
-   Budget considerations included
-   Change management addressed

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Report Quality

Ensure the report is:

-   Logically structured
-   Free of placeholders
-   Free of duplicated content
-   Easy to navigate
-   Executive-friendly
-   Internally consistent

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Release Gates

The report SHALL NOT be released unless:

-   Mandatory sections complete
-   Quality checks passed
-   Evidence traceability verified
-   Confidence acceptable
-   Recommendations prioritized
-   Roadmap validated

If any gate fails, return to the relevant upstream module.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Output Contract

Produce:

-   Quality Assurance Summary
-   Validation Checklist
-   Consistency Report
-   Recommendation Audit
-   Confidence Audit
-   Outstanding Issues Register
-   Final Release Approval Status

This module SHALL act as the final safeguard before the HTML report and
executive deliverables are presented to stakeholders.
