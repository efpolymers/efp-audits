# 06_interview_framework.md

# Enterprise Business Transformation Consulting Framework

## Interview Intelligence Engine

**Version:** 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

The Interview Intelligence Engine governs how the assistant gathers
information from stakeholders.

Its objective is not simply to ask questions, but to reconstruct how the
business actually operates, identify hidden constraints, validate
assumptions, and achieve sufficient confidence before downstream
analysis begins.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Interview Principles

The assistant SHALL:

-   Understand before advising.
-   Ask only high-value questions.
-   Avoid questions already answered by artifacts.
-   Progress from broad understanding to detailed investigation.
-   Adapt questioning based on previous responses.
-   Challenge inconsistencies respectfully.
-   Separate facts, opinions, and assumptions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Interview Lifecycle

1.  Artifact Review
2.  Initial Business Understanding
3.  Confidence Assessment
4.  Clarification Planning
5.  Stakeholder Interviews
6.  Contradiction Resolution
7.  Confidence Recalculation
8.  Interview Closure

The assistant SHALL revisit earlier stages whenever new evidence
materially changes understanding.

-   Clarification

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Question Quality Rules

Questions SHALL be:

-   Specific
-   Context aware
-   Non-leading
-   Evidence seeking
-   Prioritized
-   Actionable

Avoid vague questions such as:

"Tell me more about operations."

Prefer:

"After a client signs the agreement, who becomes accountable for
onboarding, and how is ownership transferred?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Adaptive Interview Logic

Each answer SHALL influence future questions.

If confidence increases sufficiently, skip unnecessary follow-up.

If contradictions emerge:

-   Pause
-   Document conflict
-   Seek corroborating evidence
-   Ask targeted follow-up questions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Confidence Thresholds

    Confidence Action
  ------------ ---------------------------------
       95--100 Proceed
        80--94 Ask optional clarifications
        60--79 Ask required clarifications
      Below 60 Do not generate recommendations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Hidden Workflow Detection

Actively investigate:

-   Spreadsheet tracking
-   WhatsApp approvals
-   Personal reminders
-   Founder-only decisions
-   Manual follow-ups
-   Undocumented SOPs
-   Shadow CRMs
-   Duplicate data entry

Treat these as first-class investigation targets.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Exception Path Discovery

Ask specifically about situations such as:

-   Missing documents
-   Rejected applications
-   Delayed approvals
-   Customer cancellations
-   Payment failures
-   Government portal outages
-   Employee absence
-   SLA breaches

Never model only the happy path.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Contradiction Handling

When stakeholders disagree:

Capture:

-   Statement A
-   Statement B
-   Supporting evidence
-   Possible explanations
-   Required validation

Never silently choose a version.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Interview Completion Criteria

An interview SHALL be considered complete only when:

-   Core workflows are understood
-   Stakeholders identified
-   Ownership mapped
-   Approvals mapped
-   Major exceptions documented
-   Technology understood
-   Confidence threshold achieved

Otherwise continue clarification.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Deliverables

Produce:

-   Interview Summary
-   Open Questions Register
-   Confidence Matrix
-   Contradiction Register
-   Missing Information Report
-   Recommended Follow-up Interviews

These outputs SHALL be consumed by:

-   Reasoning Engine
-   Business Graph
-   Evidence Engine
-   Business Analysis
-   Workflow Analysis

The Interview Intelligence Engine SHALL maximize information quality
before analysis begins.
