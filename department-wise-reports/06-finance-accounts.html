Section 01 — Executive Summary

Department Overview

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The Finance & Accounts team manages corporate bookkeeping, regulatory tax filing, cost accounting, and employee expense auditing. The core 3-person team relies heavily on the Zoho Suite, but encounters massive friction in cross-system data transfer and manual reconciliations.

The audit revealed a severe structural issue in cost accounting: COGS margins fluctuate wildly because the factory lacks an integrated inventory management system. Accounts cannot track Work-in-Progress (WIP) or grade-based costs, forcing manual spreadsheet reconciliations. Furthermore, the team spends 1–2 months manually reconciling 100+ GST/TDS entries against government portals, and lacks real-time stock visibility for invoicing, requiring manual phone calls to Production for every dispatch.

Section 03 — Process Detail

Current Workflows

Where each finding sits inside the operational process

Order Billing & Dispatch Invoicing — P-03

What is good: The sequence from PO receipt to invoicing is clearly defined and strictly enforced by the Accounts team.

What needs to be fixed: A blind spot in Zoho Inventory breaks the digital flow, forcing Accounts back to phone calls to ensure physical stock is available before raising the invoice.

Bottlenecks: Manual phone verification with Production creates an invoicing bottleneck and prevents seamless order dispatch.

Dependencies: Accounts is entirely reliant on Production for real-time stock updates and Sales for correct GST details.

Inventory Valuation & Costing — P-01

What is good: The team strictly follows the FIFO (First-In, First-Out) method to calculate raw material exit costs.

What needs to be fixed: Due to a lack of grade-based tracking and unamortized packaging expenses, the final recorded COGS swings violently, preventing accurate P&L forecasting.

Bottlenecks: Zoho Books cannot natively track the 3 states of raw material (Unprocessed, WIP, Ready). This requires manual spreadsheet intervention that takes 15% of the team's time.

Dependencies: Accounts is dependent on Production for WIP volume data and Logistics for transit/in-transit stock data.

Expense Reimbursement & Recon — P-02

What is good: A strict 2-level managerial approval ensures business purpose validation before expenses hit the ledger.

What needs to be fixed: Without system access for field workers, claims flow through WhatsApp, forcing managers into manual distance-checks. 100+ monthly claims take 1-2 months to reconcile against government portals.

Bottlenecks: Checking odometer readings and matching GST/TDS invoices against portal data is a massive manual time sink.

Dependencies: Accounts is dependent on Sales/BD for clear bill photographs and manual text entries of distances traveled.

Monthly CXO & Investor Reporting — P-06

What is good: The team maintains a rigorous monthly schedule for P&L and expense reporting to CXOs and investors.

What needs to be fixed: Zoho Books only reports sales by value, not by tonnage/SKUs. Accounts must manually map item counts to weights and compile it in Excel.

Bottlenecks: No real-time visibility exists. Leadership cannot track budget vs. actual expenses or target sales vs. achieved sales until the month-end report is generated.

Dependencies: Complete P&L reporting depends on the delayed reconciliation of COGS and expense claims.

Section 06 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommended Solution Segment

This section outlines the integrated technology solution proposed to resolve operational bottlenecks, replacing the legacy recommendations register.

Implementation Required

Solution Modules to be Built:

1
Accounts Frontend Dashboard
  • Real-Time Stock & WIP Sync: Connects directly to Production & Logistics Google Sheets. Accounts gets live visibility of raw material states (Unprocessed, WIP, Ready, Transit) without phone calls.
  • Unified CXO View: Integrates Zoho Books, Expense, and Payroll into one dashboard. Gives CXOs real-time P&L, budget vs. actuals, and target vs. achieved sales.
  • Automated Tonnage Calc: Custom logic layer that maps Zoho item counts to KGs/MTs, eliminating manual Excel compilation.
2
AI & Reconciliation Systems
  • GST/TDS Recon Bot: An AI agent that reads Zoho Books data and government portal exports, automatically flagging mismatches and red-flagging non-reconciled entries. Crucially, this reduces a 1-2 month process into a few hours of reviewing exceptions.
  • Smart Bill Scanner: AI layer that ingests WhatsApp/Drive bill images, extracts vendor/GST/amount data, and auto-populates the directory and Zoho Expense.
3
Automated Comms & Directory
  • Vendor & Client MDM: A central directory storing GST, addresses, and billing details. Reusable for repeat orders.
  • WhatsApp API Alerts: Automated notifications to Sales/Logistics when invoices are cleared, and automated reminders to Prod for WIP updates.

What Remains the Same

  • No New Backend Database: Zoho Books/Expense remain the core ledger. Production keeps using Google Sheets.
  • Familiar Workflows: Field staff can still submit via WhatsApp; the AI handles the structuring.

Tech Stack Utilized

  • Frontend: ReactJS + TypeScript
  • APIs: Google Sheets, Zoho Books/Expense, WhatsApp
  • AI: Gemini/LLMs for Recon & OCR extraction
  • Host: Google Cloud Platform

Key Operational Problems Solved

  • Eliminates 1-2 months of manual GST/TDS reconciliation via AI exception flagging.
  • Resolves the COGS volatility by providing live WIP visibility from Production.
  • Removes manual phone calls for stock checks and accelerates the invoicing cycle.
  • Provides CXOs and investors with real-time financial visibility without manual month-end compilation.

Future Scope of Improvement / Enhancement

  • Automated Asset Register: In the future, an AI agent could be added to actively track machinery depreciation and auto-post journal entries to Zoho Books based on invoice scans. This is not needed right now but is a planned future enhancement.

Next Steps for Team Adoption

  • Mandatory Training: Team adoption and training sessions must be made compulsory to ensure all team members understand how to use the dashboard and AI tools.
  • Feedback Loop: Establish a continuous feedback loop to iterate on the solution on the ground, ensuring it genuinely adapts to the team's operational reality and achieves actual adoption.
