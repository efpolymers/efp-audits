Section 04

Information Gaps & Cross-Department Leaks

⚠

BD ↔ Accounts (Payment & Collections)

NGO receivables at 60–70 days and Cocopeat CargoX gating both stem from weak coordination between BD and Accounts. No automated payment alerts or parallel approval workflows exist.

⚠

BD ↔ Logistics (Order Data Quality)

Incomplete WhatsApp order posts from BD to Logistics cause 15–30 min delays per order. No structured order intake form exists.

⚠

BD ↔ R&D (Trial Data)

BD accesses R&D trial data from a shared Google Drive but has no structured request mechanism. Custom trial parameters require manual R&D consultation each time.

⚠

Cross-Vertical (Customer Data)

Customer records are fragmented across 5+ spreadsheets. A customer that interacts with multiple verticals may have duplicate records with conflicting data.

⚠

B2B/RCM ↔ Retail (Tertiary Data)

EFP has zero visibility into RCM's 10,000 retail stores. Demand signals from the field don't flow back to EFP for production planning.

Section 06

Proposed Solutions

Solution: Zoho CRM Implementation (All Verticals)

Deploy Zoho CRM across all 5 BD verticals with vertical-specific deal stages. Phase 1: Lead database + deal tracking. Phase 2: Automated follow-up cadences + templates. Eliminates the #1 enterprise risk.

PriorityEffortApproachVerticals
P1MediumZoho CRM — within existing ecosystemALL

Solution: Automated Collections Workflow (NGO)

Configure automated payment reminder sequences at 30, 45, and 60 day marks. Structure billing terms with early-payment incentives. Escalation alerts to AM-BD Lead automatically.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P1LowCRM + Zoho Books payment trackingReduce collection cycle from 60–70 days to 40–50 days

Solution: Parallel CargoX Approval (Cocopeat)

Re-engineer the approval workflow so CargoX document preparation happens in parallel with payment clearance, rather than sequentially. Accounts confirms payment before final submission, but prep work isn't gated.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P1LowProcess change — no new tools neededEliminates demurrage fee risk; faster export turnaround

Solution: Separate Email Domain for Cold Outreach (Cocopeat)

Set up a secondary domain for cold outbound emails with proper warm-up protocols. Primary domain reserved for client replies and internal use.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P2LowDomain setup + email automation toolProtects primary domain reputation; safer at scale

Solution: Centralized Task Board (Government)

Transition daily tasks from WhatsApp threads to Zoho Projects or a simple Kanban board. CBDO assigns tasks via the board, Government BD updates status directly.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P2LowZoho Projects — within existing ecosystemTask visibility; CBDO freed from manual WhatsApp tracking

Solution: Mobile Form for Ground Photo Sync (Government)

Deploy a mobile form (Zoho Forms or Google Forms linked to Drive) that field staff use to upload photos directly, eliminating the WhatsApp → manual download → re-upload cycle.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P2LowLightweight form — no app build neededEliminates manual photo sync loop; reduces data loss risk
Section 07

Additional Optimization Suggestions

💡 WhatsApp Chatbot for B2B/RCM Community

The team manages 2,000 WhatsApp academy contacts with 90% drop-off (only 200 active). A chatbot could automate FAQ handling, database logging, and QC feedback collection — turning passive contacts into engaged data sources.

Priority: P3Effort: MediumImpact: Community engagement from 10% → 30%+

💡 Ad-to-Lead Response Time Reduction (Cocopeat)

Inbound queries from paid ads experience a 6–10 hour response lag due to time zones. Auto-responders with pre-qualification forms could capture high-intent leads immediately.

Priority: P2Effort: LowImpact: Captures leads that currently bounce

💡 RCM Store-Level Data Sharing Exploration

Explore API integration or structured file sharing with RCM to gain visibility into the 10,000 retail stores. Even monthly aggregate data by region would dramatically improve demand planning.

Priority: P3Effort: Medium (requires RCM cooperation)Impact: Demand forecasting accuracy

💡 Company-Owned SIM Policy for Field Staff

Transition all customer-facing BD roles to company-owned SIMs. When an employee leaves, the SIM and contact history remain with the company.

Priority: P2Effort: LowImpact: Protects ~90% of customer communications
