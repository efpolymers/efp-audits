Section 01 — Executive Summary

Vertical Overview — Government

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The Government vertical facilitates government-sponsored crop trials (e.g., UK Govt Project), tracks billing compliance, gathers field usecase data, and maintains custom trial tracking software in collaboration with external developers.

Operated by BD Executive Shailendra Singh (reporting directly to CBDO Ankit Jain), the vertical suffers from unstructured task management and high manual overhead. Daily operations, to-do lists, and field media collection run entirely through WhatsApp, leading to potential data loss and inefficient handoffs with Accounts and Logistics.

Section 02 — Risk & Control Findings

Findings Register

Ranked by risk rating. Each finding is cross-referenced to its recommendation in Section 04.
IDFindingRiskEvidence / ImpactLinked Rec.
F-01Unstructured Task Management. Daily tasks and to-dos are managed entirely through WhatsApp.HighTasks slip through cracks; relies on manual tracking by the CBDO.R-01
F-02Lack of Calendar-Leave Automation. Leave and travel schedules do not sync automatically with Google Calendar.HighIntroduces schedule coordination risks when aligning travel with regional Team Leads.R-02
F-03Manual Operations Coordinates. Invoice and order details are handed off manually via calls and WhatsApp to Accounts/Logistics.MediumHigh risk of miscommunication or data loss in inter-departmental workflows.R-03
F-04Manual Ground Media Sync. Field photos sent via WhatsApp must be manually downloaded and re-uploaded to Google Drive.LowCreates a massive administrative overhead for compiling daily trial progress.R-04
This vertical demonstrates severe workflow isolation. Heavy reliance on WhatsApp for everything—from daily tasks to invoice handoffs to field photo collection—prevents systemic visibility. By centralizing tasks in a project management tool and leveraging Zoho Forms/APIs for direct media uploads from the field to Drive, we can eliminate the manual "WhatsApp-to-Drive" shuffle and drastically improve cross-departmental coordination with Accounts and Logistics.

Section 04 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommendations Register

Ordered by priority. Effort and impact are directional estimates for planning purposes.
IDRecommendationFindingPriorityEffortExpected Impact
R-01Centralize Task Tracking — Transition daily tasks from WhatsApp threads to a dedicated project management board (e.g., Zoho Projects or ClickUp) to give CBDO clear visibility.F-01P1LowPrevents task loss and clarifies daily priorities.
R-02Automate On-Ground Photo Sync — Deploy mobile forms (e.g., Zoho Forms) to allow field staff to upload photos directly to Google Drive folders, eliminating WhatsApp downloading.F-04P1MediumSaves ~10 hours/week of manual administrative overhead.
R-03Automated HR Sync — Configure Zoho People to automatically block Google Calendars for approved leave and travel, ensuring field schedules are globally visible.F-02P2LowPrevents coordination conflicts on ground.
R-04Structured Cross-Department Handoffs — Replace WhatsApp invoice handoffs with a shared system trigger to automatically notify Accounts and Logistics when a milestone is met.F-03P2MediumReduces risk of missed billings or dispatch delays.
