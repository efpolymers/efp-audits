Section 01 — Executive Summary

Vertical Overview — Seed

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The Seed vertical is an institutional B2B sales division focused on pan-India delivery of polymer-based seed coatings and soil additives. Sales are heavily demo-led, requiring on-ground trials with seed companies, universities, and agricultural institutions.

Run entirely by a single BD Manager (Sameer Vishnuji Mahiskar), the vertical operates under severe capacity constraints. The manager spends 15–20 days a month on physical travel, leaving minimal time for lead generation, which currently relies on manual ChatGPT and Google News searches. With no CRM, follow-ups depend on memory and local ad-hoc spreadsheets.

A critical theme in this vertical is the failure to leverage existing internal data to accelerate sales. Proposal creation takes 1-2.5 days because the BD Manager manually pulls trial data from R&D’s shared Google Drive and builds custom slides. By establishing a direct, structured pipeline of R&D data into standardized sales templates—and formalizing cross-departmental alignment—this turnaround time could be reduced to hours.
