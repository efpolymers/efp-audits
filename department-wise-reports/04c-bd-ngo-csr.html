Section 01 — Executive Summary

Vertical Overview — NGO/CSR

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The NGO/CSR vertical focuses on securing high-volume bulk contracts with non-governmental organizations and corporate social responsibility departments managing agricultural and watershed projects.

The team comprises 4 active members (with 1 vacancy), split between lead generation and on-ground field trials. The vertical struggles with manual sourcing workflows (scrolling personal LinkedIn feeds) and a severely delayed payment collection cycle (60-70 days), pulling leadership focus away from BD and into manual chasing.

Section 02 — Risk & Control Findings

Findings Register

Ranked by risk rating. Each finding is cross-referenced to its recommendation in Section 04.
IDFindingRiskEvidence / ImpactLinked Rec.
F-01Extended Receivables Cycle. Accounts receivable sits at a 60-70 day delay on average for NGO clients.HighLocks up cash flow and requires the AM-BD lead to dedicate 15-20% of their time strictly to chasing payments manually.R-01
F-02Ad-Hoc Follow-Up Cadence. The team follows up every 4-5 days based on memory and a manual Google Sheet structure.MediumHigh likelihood of leads dropping out of the pipeline due to human error.R-02
F-03Sourcing Scaling Constraint. Sourcing relies heavily on organically scrolling a personal LinkedIn feed.MediumLimits the volume and consistency of top-of-funnel lead generation.R-03
F-04Manual Broadcomms. Agronomy reports and newsletters are forwarded manually to 20-30 contacts on WhatsApp.LowInefficient use of time; creates a bottleneck for maintaining relationship touchpoints.R-04
The 60-70 day collection cycle is the critical failure point in this vertical, creating a massive cash flow drag. The BD team is absorbing the collections workload manually because there is no automated synchronization with the Finance/Accounts department. Implementing a shared, automated 30/45/60-day payment reminder sequence driven by Accounts data is essential. This represents a prime opportunity for cross-departmental systems integration to relieve the BD Lead of manual dunning tasks.
