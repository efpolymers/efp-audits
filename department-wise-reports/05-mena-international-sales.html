Section 01 — Executive Summary

Department Overview

Scope, current state, and headline findings

MENA & International Sales manages B2B distributor relationships across Asia, Africa, and Australia, operating a distributor/importer-led model with direct B2B accounts in parallel. Neha Pathak reports directly to the COO and functions as the single point of contact (SPOC) for all international customers — a structure that maintains relationship quality but concentrates operational risk on one person.

The review identified four findings, of which one is rated Critical. The most material is a single-point logistics constraint that exposes international export SLAs to failure risk during peak periods. Four recommendations are set out in Section 04, sequenced across a three-phase roadmap in Section 06.

Section 02 — Risk & Control Findings

Findings Register

Ranked by risk rating. Each finding is cross-referenced to its recommendation in Section 04.
IDFindingRiskEvidence / ImpactLinked Rec.
F-01 Single-point logistics constraint. All international dispatches share one Logistics Manager (Nihal) with domestic and B2B operations. No export priority routing exists. Critical On international dispatch days, 100% of logistics capacity is consumed. Domestic orders delayed; demurrage and SLA breach risk during peak export cycles. R-01
F-02 No CRM or structured pipeline tracking. International distributor pipeline is managed entirely in Google Sheets with no stage-gating, no deal history, and no visibility for the COO. Medium Pipeline status is opaque above SPOC level; no basis for revenue forecasting or distributor performance review. R-04
F-03 No automated cross-border remittance alerts. International payments do not trigger notifications in Zoho Books; BD must manually warn Accounts before dispatch can proceed. Medium Manual advance warning required per shipment; preventable dispatch holds with low-effort automation. R-02
F-04 International client relationships on personal phone. Middle East and China accounts are maintained via a personal SIM and WeChat; employee exit risks full loss of account contacts. Low No company-owned record of international contact relationships. Full account contact list at personal risk on exit. R-03
One of four findings is Critical and is a shared-resource dependency — it cannot be resolved within MENA alone and requires formal agreement with Logistics. The two Medium findings (F-02, F-03) are self-contained quick wins. Phase 1 of the roadmap targets F-03 and F-04 as zero-build quick wins before the structural logistics and CRM work in Phase 2.

Section 03 — Process Detail

Current Workflows

Where each finding sits inside the operational process

Client Onboarding & Trial Lifecycle

Indian dosage calculators are not used for international trials. Each trial requires custom R&D-designed parameters — extending the onboarding cycle to 2–4 months per market.

Commercial Order & Shipping — F-01 F-03

Dual exposure: ~10% PO error rate causes rework before dispatch; a single shared Logistics Manager creates capacity failure during concurrent domestic and export cycles.

SPOC Communication Model

Production, Logistics, and Accounts do not contact customers directly — routing through SPOC maintains consistency but any absence creates a complete communication blackout for all active accounts.

Section 04 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommendations Register

Ordered by priority. Effort and impact are directional estimates for planning purposes.
IDRecommendationFindingPriorityEffortExpected Impact
R-01 Logistics priority routing & capacity planning — define pre-scheduled export dispatch windows; evaluate a dedicated export logistics resource or cross-training for peak periods. F-01 P1 Medium Eliminates SLA breach and demurrage risk; domestic orders no longer deprioritized during export cycles
R-02 Automated cross-border payment alerts — configure Zoho Books notifications to trigger when international payments are received, alerting Accounts and Logistics automatically without BD intervention. F-03 P1 Low Eliminates dispatch holds from manual remittance notifications; saves ~30 min per shipment

Scope as part of the enterprise CRM rollout, not a standalone implementation. F-02 P2 Low (config) Full international pipeline visibility; COO-level reporting; replaces Google Sheets tracking

R-06 Targeted outbound strategy redesign — replace generic cold outreach with exhibition-sourced contacts and trade body referrals; create region-specific technical case studies as collateral. F-02 P3 Medium Reduces inbound dependency; improves outbound conversion rate; diversifies revenue sources
