Section 01 — Executive Summary

Vertical Overview — MENA & International

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The MENA & International Sales vertical manages B2B distributor relationships across Asia, Africa, and Australia. Operating as a distributor/importer-led model, the vertical is led by Neha Pathak, who acts as the single point of contact (SPOC) for all international customers.

The audit identified three compounding structural failures: a single-point logistics constraint sharing one manager with domestic ops, a manual cross-border remittance workflow requiring BD to warn Accounts before dispatch, and international client data trapped on personal SIMs/WeChat. Furthermore, the pipeline is managed entirely in disconnected Google Sheets with no CRM visibility for the COO. The vertical scores 2.0 maturity against a target of 3.1.

Section 03 — Process Detail

Current Workflows

Where each finding sits inside the operational process

Client Onboarding & Trial Lifecycle

What is good: The trial lifecycle is comprehensive and logically sequenced. Indian dosage calculators are bypassed in favor of custom R&D parameters, ensuring scientific accuracy for international climates.

What needs to be fixed: Every trial requires ad-hoc R&D consultation. There is no standardized intake form for international trial requests, adding 3–5 back-and-forth emails per initiation.

Bottlenecks: The 2–4 month crop cycle is scientifically necessary, but the setup phase is artificially inflated by unstructured intake.

Dependencies: R&D must manually review and design parameters for every new international trial request received from Neha.

Commercial Order & Shipping — P-01 P-03

What is good: The PO completeness rate is high (~90%), requiring corrections on only 1 in 10 orders before invoicing.

What needs to be fixed: International payments do not trigger Zoho Books alerts. BD must manually warn Accounts before dispatch can proceed. Furthermore, export dispatch is routed to a Logistics Manager shared with domestic ops.

Bottlenecks: On international dispatch days, 100% of logistics capacity is consumed. This delays domestic orders and creates demurrage and SLA breach risk during peak export cycles.

Dependencies: Critical dependency on the Logistics Manager (shared resource) and Finance/Accounts for manual payment clearance.

SPOC Communication Model & Data Risk — P-02 P-04

What is good: Routing through a SPOC maintains relationship quality and consistency for international distributors across time zones.

What needs to be fixed: Middle East and China accounts are maintained via a personal SIM and WeChat. There is no company-owned record of international contact relationships.

Bottlenecks: If Neha is unavailable, there is a complete communication blackout for all active accounts. Employee exit risks full loss of account contacts.

Dependencies: Internal teams (Production, Logistics, Accounts) are entirely dependent on Neha to relay customer context and trial updates.

Section 06 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommended Solution Segment

This section outlines the integrated technology solution proposed to resolve operational bottlenecks, replacing the legacy recommendations register. Built on the Solution Design Framework — extend existing Zoho Books/CRM, secure data on existing channels, integrate cross-departmentally.

Implementation Required

Solution Modules to be Built:

1
Ops & Finance Automation (Integration)
  • Zoho Books API Alerts: Configure automated cross-border payment notifications. When an international payment clears, Zoho Books triggers an alert directly to Accounts and Logistics via WhatsApp/Slack—eliminating manual BD warnings.
  • Logistics Dispatch Scheduler: A shared dashboard module visualizing export vs. domestic dispatch capacity. Forces pre-scheduled export windows so international SLAs don't cannibalize domestic capacity.
2
Secure Comms & CRM Pipeline (Extend)
  • Virtual Business Number: Transition Middle East and China comms from Neha's personal SIM/WeChat to a company-owned Virtual Business Line. Secures all contact IP against employee turnover.
  • Zoho CRM Migration: Replace the Google Sheets pipeline tracker with Zoho CRM (scoped within enterprise rollout). Provides the COO with live deal-stage visibility and distributor performance tracking.
3
Outbound & Trial Intake Infrastructure (Custom Build)
  • Standardized R&D Trial Form: Structured digital intake (crop type, region, climate, target outcomes) replaces ad-hoc email consultations for every new international trial. Reduces per-trial setup time by ~40%.
  • Targeted Outbound Engine: Implement automated lead scraping (Apollo/LinkedIn Sales Navigator) integrated with the CRM, paired with region-specific technical case studies to boost outbound conversion.

What Remains the Same

  • No New Finance Tool: Zoho Books remains the system of record. We simply configure its native API alert rules.
  • Familiar Channels: The team continues using WhatsApp/Slack/WeChat. The transition is backend (Personal → Company Virtual Line).

Tech Stack Utilized

  • Frontend: ReactJS + TypeScript (Dashboard)
  • APIs: Zoho Books, Zoho CRM, WhatsApp Business
  • AI: Apollo / LinkedIn Sales Navigator
  • Host: Google Cloud Platform

Key Operational Problems Solved

  • Eliminates manual payment warnings and dispatch holds via Zoho Books API integration.
  • Secures 100% of international client data against employee turnover via Virtual Business Lines.
  • Provides COO with live CRM pipeline visibility, replacing monthly manual Google Sheets updates.
  • Accelerates trial setup by 40% through structured R&D intake forms, replacing ad-hoc email chains.

Future Scope of Improvement / Enhancement

  • Dedicated Export Logistics Role: If export volume scales beyond the capacity of the shared Logistics Manager, a dedicated export coordinator may be required. The Phase 1 dashboard will provide the data to justify this headcount.

Next Steps for Team Adoption

  • SOP Update: Accounts and Logistics must update their clearance SOPs to rely on the automated Zoho Books alert rather than Neha's manual warning.
  • CRM Training: Neha must complete Zoho CRM walkthroughs. Adoption of the CRM is mandatory before outbound lead-gen tools are layered on.
  • Device Handoff: Formalize the transition of international contacts to the company Virtual Business Line; personal numbers phased out.
