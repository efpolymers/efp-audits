Section 03

Challenges, Risks & Red Flags

1. Single-Point Logistics ConstraintCRITICAL

International dispatches depend entirely on a single Logistics Manager (Nihal) who is shared across domestic, B2B, and export segments. During peak export cycles, shipping delays are inevitable.

MetricDetail
Time WasteInternational dispatch days consume 100% of Logistics Manager's time
InvestmentMedium — additional logistics personnel or process streamlining
ROIVery High — prevents export SLA breaches; avoids demurrage fees
Cross-DepartmentLogistics, Production, All BD verticals
2. Ineffective Outbound Sales FunnelMEDIUM

Cold outreach via email and LinkedIn has very low conversion rates. Global revenue is primarily reliant on inbound inquiries, creating a growth bottleneck.

MetricDetail
Time WasteHours spent on low-conversion outbound channels
InvestmentMedium — targeted outbound strategy + email automation
ROIMedium — diversifies revenue sources beyond inbound dependency
3. Delayed Cross-Border Remittance AlertsMEDIUM

Cross-border bank receipts do not trigger instant alerts in Zoho Books. BD must manually warn Accounts before dispatches can proceed — creating a preventable hold.

MetricDetail
Time WasteManual advance warnings per payment; dispatch holds
InvestmentLow — bank API or Zoho Books notification configuration
ROIHigh — eliminates manual payment tracking; faster dispatch clearance
4. Personal Phone DependencyLOW

International client relationships in the Middle East and China are maintained on a personal phone number. Employee exit risks loss of all account contacts.

MetricDetail
InvestmentMinimal — company SIM or virtual business number
ROIHigh relative to cost — protects critical international relationships
Section 04

Information Gaps & Cross-Department Leaks

⚠

MENA ↔ Logistics (Shared Resource Bottleneck)

International dispatches share a single Logistics Manager with domestic and B2B operations. During peak export periods, domestic orders are delayed and vice versa. No priority routing exists.

⚠

MENA ↔ Accounts (Payment Visibility)

Cross-border payments don't trigger automatic alerts. BD must manually inform Accounts of incoming payments to prevent dispatch holds.

⚠

MENA ↔ R&D (Trial Parameters)

Each international trial requires custom R&D-designed parameters. No standardized request form exists — each request is a manual consultation.

Section 06

Proposed Solutions

Solution: Logistics Priority Routing & Capacity Planning

Define priority rules for logistics resource allocation during peak periods. International export dispatches should have pre-scheduled windows. Evaluate hiring an additional logistics resource or cross-training existing personnel.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P1MediumProcess rules + potential hiringEliminates shipping SLA breaches; prevents demurrage

Solution: Automated Cross-Border Payment Alerts

Configure Zoho Books to trigger automatic notifications when international payments are received. BD and Logistics are alerted instantly — no manual advance warnings needed.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P1LowZoho Books notification config or bank APIEliminates dispatch holds from payment visibility gaps

Solution: Company SIM for International BD Manager

Issue a company-owned SIM or virtual business number for international client communications. Separates personal and professional channels.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P2MinimalSIM/virtual number setupProtects international account relationships

Solution: Standardized R&D Trial Request Form

Create a structured form for BD to submit trial requests to R&D, including crop type, region, climate parameters, and target outcomes. Reduces back-and-forth consultations.

PriorityEffortApproachExpected Impact
P2LowForm template — no system build neededFaster trial setup; reduced R&D consultation time
Section 07

Additional Optimization Suggestions

💡 CRM Module for MENA (Part of Enterprise CRM)

The department requires a CRM with parent "Company" records and nested "Deal/Crop Accounts" for parallel trial tracking. This should be configured as a custom module within the enterprise Zoho CRM rollout.

Priority: P1 (enterprise CRM)Impact: Full international pipeline visibility

💡 Targeted Outbound Strategy Redesign

Rather than generic cold outreach, focus outbound on exhibition-sourced contacts and trade body referrals. Create region-specific case studies and technical one-pagers as outreach collateral.

Priority: P3Effort: MediumImpact: Improves outbound conversion
