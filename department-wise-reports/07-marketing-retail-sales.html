Section 01 — Executive Summary

Department Overview

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The Marketing & Retail Sales department is fundamentally a field execution and tracking unit. Led by Gaurav Jain, the team builds monthly MIS Sales Plans and orchestrates a distributed field force to execute dealer and farmer visits.

The core bottleneck is the department's heavy reliance on WhatsApp and manual data-entry layers for critical operations. Field force geolocation tracking, Purchase Orders (POs), and payment confirmations all flow through unstructured WhatsApp messages. The headquarters team spends upwards of 6–8 hours daily manually verifying salesman locations and transcribing PO data, creating a fragile and unscalable operational ceiling.

Section 02 — Risk & Control Findings

Findings Register

Ranked by risk rating. Each finding is cross-referenced to its recommendation in Section 04.
IDFindingRiskEvidence / ImpactLinked Rec.
F-01 Manual Field Geolocation Tracking. HQ team must manually cross-reference WhatsApp live locations against home addresses three times a day. Critical Burns 6-8 hours/day of executive time on basic compliance rather than sales enablement. R-01
F-02 Inefficient Sales Actuals Extraction. Tracking targets against actuals requires manually pulling customer-wise reports from Zoho Books. Critical Lag in pipeline visibility; MIS plans are always backward-looking. R-02
F-03 Analog PO and Quotation Verification. Field reps write POs on paper and send WhatsApp photos, requiring HQ to re-type data. Medium High risk of transcription error; slows down order-to-dispatch timeline. R-03
F-04 Friction-Prone Payment Confirmations. Payment screenshots sent via WhatsApp must be manually coordinated with Accounts. Medium Delays shipment release authorizations while waiting on cross-department checks. R-05
F-05 Siloed Marketing Collateral Audits. Printed stock is managed in isolated sheets without automated minimum levels. Low Risk of collateral stockouts during critical regional campaigns. R-04
The two Critical findings expose how analog tracking creates an unscalable operational ceiling. F-01 (Manual Geolocation) forces the HQ team to act as surveillance monitors rather than sales strategists, burning 6-8 hours daily. F-02 (Actuals Extraction) means MIS plans are perpetually backward-looking. Phase 1 targets the structural tracking gap by deploying an automated field force app and syncing actuals via API, instantly recovering massive HQ capacity.

Section 04 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommendations Register

Ordered by priority. Effort and impact are directional estimates for planning purposes.
IDRecommendationFindingPriorityEffortExpected Impact
R-01 Automated Field Tracking App — Procure a SaaS tool (e.g., Locus, FieldAssist) to geo-fence attendance, completely replacing the WhatsApp 3x-daily manual check. F-01 P1 High Frees up 6-8 hours daily for the HQ team, redirecting effort to sales analysis.
R-02 Zoho API Sync to MIS — Utilize API connections to push actual invoiced metrics from Zoho Books directly into the MIS Google Sheet automatically. F-02 P1 Medium Provides real-time visibility into target completion without manual data dumps.
R-03 Mobile Intake Forms — Transition field reps from hand-written paper POs to digital intake forms (via smartphone) that feed directly into the central sheet. F-03 P2 Medium Cuts out manual transcription and prevents order details from getting lost in WhatsApp.
R-04 Centralized Payment Clearing — Integrate a read-only payment clearance view from Accounts directly into the MIS tracker. F-04 P2 Medium Speeds up shipment authorizations by removing cross-department chat pinging.
R-05 Automated Collateral Alerts — Set conditional formatting/triggers on the Marketing Master Sheet to flag when inventory dips below minimum MOQ levels. F-05 P3 Low Prevents campaign delays due to stockouts of printed materials.
