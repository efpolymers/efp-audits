6–8 hours/day of staff time consumed by manual WhatsApp-based location verification. This is the department's single largest operational burden.

Section 04

Information Gaps & Cross-Department Leaks

⚠

Marketing ↔ Accounts (Payment Verification)

Payment confirmations rely on WhatsApp screenshots sent to Accounts for manual verification. No automated payment alert system exists.

⚠

Marketing ↔ Production (Stock Visibility)

Retail sales team has no visibility into production stock levels. Must check with Accounts who in turn call Production — creating a 2-hop information delay.

⚠

Field ↔ R&D (Farmer Feedback)

Ground-level farmer feedback and product performance data from field visits does not systematically flow to R&D for product improvement.

Section 06

Proposed Solutions

Solution: Automated Field Force Tracking (Offline-Capable)

Deploy Zoho FSM or equivalent with offline GPS capture. Auto-syncs when connectivity returns. Completely eliminates manual WhatsApp-based tracking.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1MediumRecovers 6–8 hrs/day; nearly one FTE equivalent freed

Solution: Digital Mobile PO Forms

Replace paper PO books with a mobile form (Zoho Forms or custom). RSMs fill in structured fields on their phone — data flows directly to Accounts for invoicing.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1LowSaves 15–20 min/PO; eliminates transcription errors

Solution: UTM Tracking + Marketing Analytics

Implement UTM parameters on all ad campaigns. Connect to Zoho Analytics for conversion tracking. Monthly ROI review with COO.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P2LowMakes marketing spend accountable; identifies what works

Solution: CRM-Based Target Tracking

Use Zoho CRM's target module for quantity-level target vs. actual tracking. Replaces manual Sheet reconciliation.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P2Low (part of CRM rollout)Saves 3–4 hrs/week; real-time target tracking
