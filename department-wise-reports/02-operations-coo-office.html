Section 01 — Executive Summary

Department Overview

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The COO Office exercises broad strategic oversight spanning Finance, HR, Marketing, Retail Sales, and MENA business development. While direct execution is delegated, the COO actively dictates annual planning, manually reconciles finance reports, takes 60-80 cultural fit interviews per role, and resolves daily operational friction across fragmented tools.

The audit uncovered severe operational saturation. The COO manages 50–80 daily emails and 150+ WhatsApp groups without a unified dashboard. In HR, critical processes like PMS, L&D, and offer approvals are buried in email and Google Sheets. In Finance, monthly reporting requires manual extraction from Zoho Books rather than real-time dashboard visibility. Retail Sales data is entirely haywired on WhatsApp, and MENA lacks a centralized CRM to track complex trial lifecycles.

Section 03 — Process Detail (Part 1)

Current Workflows: HR & Finance

Where each finding sits inside the operational process

HR Hiring & Cultural Fit Funnel — P-04

What needs to be fixed: The COO spends massive bandwidth conducting 18-20 cultural fit interviews per role (30-40 mins each) and manually approving offer letters over email. There is no ATS dashboard for pending JDs, interview notes, or offer approvals.

Detailed Workflow Steps:
1. Requisition: Hiring department submits staffing requests via Google Forms.
2. HR Screening: HR Manager (Dolly Mehta) screens 60-80 candidate resumes per position.
3. HOD Interview: Department HODs conduct technical rounds and shortlist 15-20 candidates.
4. COO Fit Interview: COO (Puran Singh Rajput) personally interviews all 15-20 candidates per role.
5. Offer Release: Offer letters are drafted and approved manually via email threads.

Cross-Department Involvement: HR & Talent (Dolly Mehta - Resume Screening & Admin), Hiring HODs (Technical Evaluation), and COO Office (Culture Fit & Offer Approval).

Dependencies & Bottlenecks: Over-involvement of COO in non-executive interviews creates severe hiring lag. HR relies on manual email sign-offs without centralized candidate tracking.

Finance & Expense Reconciliation — P-05

What needs to be fixed: Absence of a real-time finance dashboard forces the team to manually download Zoho reports and recalculate. Expense approvals lack automated policy validation, causing budget over-expenditure.

Detailed Workflow Steps:
1. Claim Submission: Staff across all verticals log 100+ monthly travel and operational claims.
2. Manager Sign-off: Line managers approve claims without automated policy verification against policy limits.
3. Data Extraction: Finance Head (Shobha Shekhawat) exports raw CSV transaction logs from Zoho Books.
4. Spreadsheet Reconciliation: Accounts team and COO manually recalculate tax offsets and category budgets in Excel.
5. Board Reporting: Consolidated figures are manually transcribed into static executive presentations.

Cross-Department Involvement: All Operating Units (Claim Submission), Finance & Accounts (Shobha Shekhawat - Entry & Audit), and COO Office (Expense Governance & P&L Review).

Dependencies & Bottlenecks: Finance depends on manual spreadsheet manipulation, delaying executive financial visibility and risking policy non-compliance.

Section 03 — Process Detail (Part 2)

Current Workflows: Retail, MENA & Board

Retail Field Tracking via WhatsApp — P-02

What needs to be fixed: Entire retail field force operates on unstructured WhatsApp groups. Travel mileage, farmer contacts, and field demo results are buried in chat logs with zero CRM structure.

Detailed Workflow Steps:
1. Field Execution: Retail Sales Manager (Gaurav Jain) and field reps conduct visits in UP, MP, and Rajasthan.
2. Chat Logging: Reps post unformatted visit images, receipts, and text notes into 150+ shared WhatsApp groups.
3. Management Review: COO and Sales Lead manually skim chats to verify daily rep activity.
4. Information Decay: Critical farmer leads, demo conversion metrics, and field ROI disappear in scroll history.

Cross-Department Involvement: Retail Sales Force (Gaurav Jain & Field Reps), Agronomy & Marketing (Field Demos), and COO Office (Operational Oversight).

Dependencies & Bottlenecks: Management relies on manual chat scanning to track field team productivity. Needs an AI WhatsApp bot parser and field CRM.

MENA Pipeline & Trial Lifecycle — P-06

What needs to be fixed: Complex MENA B2B trials (parent accounts, multi-crop trials in UAE/KSA) are managed entirely on WhatsApp. A partitioned CRM is needed so CXOs can monitor live trial stages and R&D handoffs.

Detailed Workflow Steps:
1. Lead Intake: Director – MENA (Neha Pathak) receives international distributor and enterprise trial requests.
2. Sample Shipping: Product samples are dispatched from India manufacturing to MENA clients.
3. R&D Protocol: R&D team establishes crop-specific agronomy trial protocols and monitoring parameters.
4. Trial Tracking: Field performance photos and updates are logged informally on WhatsApp chats.
5. Commercial Closure: Contract negotiations lack stage-gated CRM pipeline visibility.

Cross-Department Involvement: MENA Sales & Agronomy (Neha Pathak - Client Lead), R&D (Ritu Panwar - Agronomy Protocols), Production & Logistics (Sample Dispatch), and COO Office (Executive Contract Approval).

Dependencies & Bottlenecks: If Neha Pathak is traveling or offline, communication stalls. Requires single CRM with department partitions.

Board/Investor Reporting Prep — P-01

What needs to be fixed: Board prep is a massive manual drain. The COO combines data from multiple sheets to create static decks. Needs a live dashboard reflecting actuals vs. projected sales/expenses, with an LLM agent to auto-generate investor reports.

Detailed Workflow Steps:
1. Reporting Trigger: Investor reporting cycles or Board meetings prompt data requests.
2. Inter-Dept Data Harvesting: COO requests operational updates, headcount figures, sales numbers, and finance metrics.
3. Manual Compilation: COO personally normalizes and combines disjointed Google Sheets and Zoho extracts.
4. Static Deck Assembly: Manual creation of PowerPoint/Google Slides presentation decks without automated metric feeds.

Cross-Department Involvement: CEO Office (Narayan Lal Gurjar - Strategic Alignment), COO Office (Puran Singh Rajput - Deck Assembly), All Department Leads (Data Submissions), and Board / Investors.

Dependencies & Bottlenecks: Consumes days of executive bandwidth prior to every board meeting. Requires automated dashboard and LLM report generator.

Section 06 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommended Solution Segment

Integrated technology solutions proposed to resolve operational bottlenecks across all COO domains.

Implementation Required

Solution Modules to be Built:

1
Unified CXO Dashboard & AI Assistant
  • Centralized Approvals: Digitize all HR (offers, policies) and Finance (expenses, budgets) approvals. Real-time visibility into pending/overdue items.
  • Knowledge Layer Chatbot: Connect all department sheets/dashboards. Replaces subpar Zoho Zia. Answers situational policy/FAQ queries instantly.
  • Email & Board Automation: AI Chrome extension to segregate inbox, flag red alerts, and suggest replies. Auto-generate investor reports via LLM based on live data.
2
Gamified HR & PMS Ecosystem
  • Performance Management: Connect KPIs/KRAs to a gamified PMS. Track 1-month/3-month evals and annual performance transparently for appraisals.
  • L&D Portal: Skill-mapping directory, training requests, and assessment criteria. Team members can find internal skill holders.
  • Automated Onboarding: Centralized e-signing for offers/NDAs. Auto-create profiles across department platforms to eliminate manual HR work.
3
WhatsApp AI Bot & Retail CRM
  • WA AI Agent: Parses haywired retail text/images (travel, expenses, farmer data) into templatized dashboard data for RSMs and CXOs.
  • Target Tracking: Field managers can query the bot for individual geography/distributor target progress.
  • Automated Onboarding: Distributor/RSM onboarding documentation handled via simple WA forms.
4
Finance Automation & MENA CRM
  • Expense Policy Bot: System check at input level to verify receipts/amounts against policies. Reduces manager verification load.
  • Tax LLM Alert: AI agent tracks government tax policy changes and alerts finance for reconciliation.
  • Unified CRM: Single CRM divided by departments. MENA gets trial tracking (R&D handoffs, follow-ups) visible to CXOs.

Key Operational Problems Solved

  • Eliminates manual email approvals and board deck preparation through live CXO dashboards and LLM reporting.
  • Transforms WhatsApp chaos into structured retail data and target tracking via AI parsing.
  • Secures transparent appraisals and skill development through a gamified PMS/L&D ecosystem.
  • Stops over-expenditure with input-level policy checks and provides live MENA trial visibility.
