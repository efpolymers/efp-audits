Section 01 — Executive Summary

Department Overview

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The COO Office manages broad strategic oversight encompassing Finance, HR, Marketing, Retail Sales compliance, and MENA business development. While direct execution is delegated, the COO actively dictates marketing projections, structures Board/Investor reporting, and resolves daily operational friction.

The review uncovered a severe communication bottleneck: managing 50–80 daily emails and 150+ WhatsApp groups has created a saturation point, relying perilously on "unread" email markers for task triage. Additionally, the lack of a dedicated Marketing Manager leaves budgets unmeasured for ROI, and the Retail Sales tracking app fails in low-network regions, bleeding critical compliance data.

Section 06 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommended Solution Segment

This section outlines the integrated technology solution proposed to resolve operational bottlenecks, replacing the legacy recommendations register.

Implementation Required

Solution Modules to be Built:

1
Centralized Communication & Task Capture
  • Executive Task Management: Deploy a structured task manager (e.g., Todoist, Asana) to replace the "unread email" tracking method. All tasks, approvals, and decisions are logged, assigned, and tracked to completion.
  • Post-Hours Idea Capture: Establish a dedicated Slack #brainstorming channel for late-night ideas with auto-capture to the task queue, ensuring no strategic thought is lost.
  • Channel Consolidation: Consolidate 151 WhatsApp groups into structured Slack channels with clear purpose definitions and archival policies.
2
Offline-First Field Force Tracking
  • Offline Data Collection: Upgrade or replace the retail field sales application to support robust offline data collection, syncing automatically when network restores.
  • Structured Farmer Feedback: Establish structured farmer feedback fields in the app to replace unformatted WhatsApp text templates.
  • Pipeline Logging: Mandate digital pipeline logging for distributors to track onboarding stages directly from the field.
3
Marketing Accountability & CRM Infrastructure
  • Marketing Manager Recruitment: Hire a dedicated Marketing Manager to decouple marketing execution from BD duties, with strict CAC/ROI metric tracking for the 25-50% budget.
  • Account-Based CRM: Deploy a CRM (e.g., HubSpot) capable of parent/child account relationships to track complex B2B trials. Integrate telecaller feedback loops and performance gamification for the Retail team.

What Remains the Same

  • Core Systems: Zoho Books and CRM remain the core financial and sales data sources.
  • Communication Habits: Slack and WhatsApp remain primary channels; the solution fits into existing habits without forcing new external software.

Tech Stack Utilized

  • Task Management: Asana / Todoist
  • Field App: React Native / Flutter (offline-first)
  • CRM: HubSpot (account-based)
  • Automation: Slack Workflow Builder, WhatsApp Business API

Key Operational Problems Solved

  • Eliminates the COO's single-point bottleneck on task triage and approvals, ensuring 100% follow-up compliance.
  • Restores data integrity to the retail pipeline with zero data loss in low-network agricultural zones.
  • Secures accountability for 25-50% marketing budget spend with strict CAC/ROI tracking.
  • Provides real-time visibility into complex B2B/MENA trial stages with parent/child account tracking.

Future Scope of Improvement / Enhancement

  • AI-Powered Triage Assistant: In the future, an AI agent could automatically categorize and prioritize incoming communications, draft responses, and flag urgent items for the COO. This is not needed right now but is a planned future enhancement.

Next Steps for Team Adoption

  • Mandatory Training: Team adoption sessions for the new task manager and field app must be compulsory to ensure all team members transition from email/WhatsApp-based tracking.
  • Feedback Loop: Establish a continuous feedback loop between the COO and field teams to iterate on the field app's offline capabilities and CRM workflow design.
