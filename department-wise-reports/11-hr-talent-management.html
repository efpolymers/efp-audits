Section 01 — Executive Summary

Department Overview

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The Human Resources department, led by Dolly Mehta, governs the entire employee lifecycle—from Q4 strategic hiring to performance appraisals, payroll, and eventual offboarding.

The audit revealed a heavily manual, disconnected ecosystem. Recruitment relies on isolated Google Sheets, creating a complete blind spot for leadership regarding pipeline health. Once hired, employees are not automatically provisioned across Zoho apps, forcing redundant data entry. Furthermore, performance appraisals remain tied to infrequent, subjective annual discussions rather than being linked to real-time, monthly KPI tracking.

Section 06 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommended Solution Segment

Architectural roadmap for operational digitalization
Module 1: Integrated Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
Addresses Findings: H-01 H-03
Proposed Implementation
Deploy a centralized ATS dashboard to replace fragmented Google Sheets for pipeline visibility.
Integrate automated offer letter generation and e-signature (NDA) workflows, routing approvals away from unstructured email.
Digitalize the "Person Required Form" via a structured intake portal for Team Leads.
Module 2: Automated Onboarding & Provisioning
Addresses Findings: H-02
Proposed Implementation
Configure automated handoffs so that once a candidate is marked "Hired" in the ATS, their Zoho People profile is auto-generated.
Trigger automatic assignment of role-specific KPIs, L&D tracks, and compliance document requests on day one.
Establish automated exit protocol blueprints to ensure secure and complete offboarding.
Module 3: Continuous Performance Management (PMS)
Addresses Findings: H-04
Proposed Implementation
Shift from annual subjective appraisals to a monthly, data-driven check-in cadence within Zoho People.
Integrate live operational data (e.g., Sales targets, QC metrics) directly into the PMS review fields.
Module 4: Centralized L&D Portal & Skills Matrix
Addresses Findings: H-05
Proposed Implementation
Develop a central skills directory ("who knows what") to map internal competencies and easily identify training gaps.
Create a formalized request portal for employees and TLs to submit structured training requests.
Implement standardized assessment forms and gamified participation tracking to measure training efficacy.
