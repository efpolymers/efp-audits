Section 03

Challenges, Risks & Red Flags

1. Lack of Applicant Tracking System (ATS)CRITICAL

The recruitment pipeline (managing 60–80 interviews for 18–20 roles) relies entirely on independent Google Sheets and Forms. There is no unified dashboard for leadership to view hiring velocity, bottleneck stages, or conversion rates.

MetricDetail
Time WasteManual tracking; scattered candidate communication; email-based approvals
InvestmentMedium — Zoho Recruit (native integration with Zoho People)
ROIHigh — Leadership visibility; faster time-to-hire; zero dropped candidates
2. Siloed Onboarding ProcessHIGH

When a candidate is hired, their transition to an employee requires manual data entry across platforms. Profiles, NDAs, L&D tracks, and KPI assignments are not auto-provisioned.

MetricDetail
Time WasteHours of manual setup per new hire
InvestmentLow — Zoho Recruit to Zoho People automated handoff configuration
ROIHigh — Error-free onboarding; seamless day-one employee experience
3. Subjective & Infrequent AppraisalsMEDIUM

Performance reviews occur annually and rely heavily on subjective manager discussions. They are disconnected from live operational KPIs (like Zoho CRM sales targets or Zoho Projects task completion).

MetricDetail
Time WasteLost performance tracking between annual cycles
InvestmentMedium — Zoho People Performance module configuration (Monthly cadence)
ROIMedium — Data-driven increments; faster course-correction for underperformers
4. Ad-Hoc L&D InfrastructureLOW

Training requests are unstructured. There is no central skills matrix ("who knows what") to match skillsets to project needs or identify systemic training gaps.

MetricDetail
InvestmentLow — Skills matrix database; structured request portal
ROIMedium — Targeted training spend; better internal mobility
Section 04

Information Gaps & Cross-Department Leaks

⚠

HR ↔ COO (Hiring Visibility)

The COO approves budgets and final hires but cannot see the mid-funnel pipeline progress because it lives in disjointed spreadsheets.

⚠

HR ↔ Team Leads (Performance Data)

Team Leads possess operational data (sales hit, project completion), but HR does not have automated access to this data for appraisal cycles, forcing manual compilation.

⚠

HR ↔ Operations (Skills Allocation)

Without a central skills inventory database, operational leaders cannot easily identify internal talent for new initiatives, leading to unnecessary external hiring requests.

Section 06

Proposed Solutions

Solution: Implement Zoho Recruit (ATS)

Deploy Zoho Recruit to centralize the hiring pipeline. This provides the COO with a live pipeline dashboard, replaces manual Google Sheets, and standardizes "Person Required" digital forms for Team Leads.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1MediumLeadership visibility; faster hiring cycles; no dropped candidates

Solution: Automated Onboarding Handoff

Configure the native integration between Zoho Recruit and Zoho People. When an offer is accepted, the system should automatically generate the employee profile, trigger NDA e-signing, and assign day-one checklists.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1LowEliminates manual profile setup; seamless day-one experience

Solution: Monthly KPI-Driven Appraisals

Shift from annual subjective reviews to a monthly cadence using Zoho People's Performance module. Integrate this module with operational data (e.g., Zoho CRM targets) so appraisals are data-backed.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P2MediumData-driven increments; real-time performance tracking

Solution: Centralized Skills Inventory Database

Design a central skills directory ("know who knows what") in Zoho People. Track employee certifications, project history, and capabilities to optimize internal resource allocation and target L&D programs.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P3LowTargeted L&D spend; faster internal project staffing
