Section 03

Challenges, Risks & Red Flags

1. Key-Person Dependency: PD ManagerCRITICAL

Protocol approvals, grant writing, technical sign-offs, and multi-geography compliance are all bottlenecked on a single individual (Ritu Panwar). Unavailability creates regulatory delays and trial backlogs across all BD verticals.

MetricDetail
Time WasteNot a time waste but a critical continuity risk — if PD Manager is unavailable, entire R&D pipeline stalls
InvestmentMedium — cross-training junior team members; documentation of protocols
ROIVery High — business continuity; prevents single-point failure on entire technical operation
Cross-DepartmentAll BD verticals (trial support), Production (QC), International (compliance)
2. Scattered R&D Data (8–10 Spreadsheets)CRITICAL

Research data is fragmented across 8–10 disconnected Google Sheets and Excel files. No centralized research database exists. This leads to redundant testing, data loss risk, and repeat trial costs.

MetricDetail
Time Waste~4–6 hrs/month aggregating data for board reports; redundant trials from undiscoverable past results
InvestmentMedium — centralized R&D database with version control
ROIHigh — prevents redundant testing; preserves institutional knowledge; faster query resolution
3. Ad-Hoc BD Trial RequestsMEDIUM

BD verticals request trial support through unstructured verbal consultations. No standardized request form captures crop type, region, climate, or target outcomes — creating back-and-forth and delays.

MetricDetail
Time Waste~30 min per trial request in back-and-forth clarifications
InvestmentLow — structured intake form
ROIMedium — faster trial setup; fewer iterations
4. Power BI Onboarding Not ConnectedLOW

Power BI is being onboarded but is not yet connected to any data source. Without integration, it will become another disconnected tool rather than a consolidation solution.

MetricDetail
InvestmentLow — connect to centralized data sources once established
ROIMedium — visualization layer for R&D data; cross-timezone dashboard for board
Section 04

Information Gaps & Cross-Department Leaks

⚠

R&D ↔ All BD (Trial Data)

BD teams access trial data from Google Drive but have no structured search mechanism. Finding relevant past trials for pitches requires manual R&D consultation.

⚠

R&D ↔ Production (Lab-to-Commercial)

Formulation handoffs lack a standardized protocol. Scaling lab formulations to commercial production is done ad-hoc, risking quality inconsistencies.

⚠

R&D ↔ Field Teams (Farmer Feedback)

Ground-level agricultural data from field operations doesn't flow back to R&D systematically. Product improvement insights are trapped in WhatsApp threads.

Section 06

Proposed Solutions

Solution: Cross-Train Junior R&D Staff

Document all protocol approval criteria, grant writing templates, and compliance checklists. Train 1–2 junior team members to handle routine approvals and compliance tasks. PD Manager retains final authority but is no longer the sole executor.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1Medium (time investment)Eliminates single-point failure; business continuity for entire technical operation

Solution: Centralized R&D Knowledge Database

Migrate 8–10 disconnected spreadsheets into a centralized, searchable database. Options: structured Google Sheets with a master index, Notion, or a custom Zoho Creator app. Include version control and search functionality.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1MediumPrevents redundant testing; preserves institutional knowledge; faster query resolution

Solution: Structured Trial Request Form

Create a standardized form for BD teams to submit trial requests with required fields: crop type, region, climate, target outcomes, sample specs. Eliminates ad-hoc consultations.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P2LowFaster trial setup; fewer iterations; clear documentation

Solution: Connect Power BI to Centralized Data

Once the R&D database is centralized, connect Power BI for visualization and cross-timezone board reporting. Eliminates 4–6 hrs/month of manual data aggregation.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P3Low–MediumAutomated R&D dashboards; real-time board visibility
