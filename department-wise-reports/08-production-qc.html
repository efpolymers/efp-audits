The team manages raw material procurement, granulation, grading, packaging, inventory tracking, and quality control processes. A custom Zoho WMS is under development to digitize factory-floor operations.

Section 03

Challenges, Risks & Red Flags

1. Zoho WMS ↔ Zoho Books DisconnectCRITICAL

The custom Zoho WMS being built is not connected to Zoho Books (the accounting ERP). This creates a data silo where production costs, stock movements, and WIP data don't flow to Finance automatically.

MetricDetail
Time WasteOngoing manual reconciliation; inaccurate production costing
InvestmentMedium — API integration during development phase (cheapest to fix now)
ROIVery High — automated cost flow; accurate COGS; audit-ready
Cross-DepartmentFinance & Accounts (primary consumer)
2. 68% Packaging SurplusHIGH

Printer MOQ (16,000) vs. actual requirement (~5,000) creates 68% surplus packaging per print run. With EFP's multi-variable product matrix (SKU × language × design), surplus pouches risk obsolescence on design changes.

MetricDetail
Time WasteWarehousing overhead; write-off risk on design changes
InvestmentMedium — flexible printer sourcing, consortium buying, or digital printing
ROIMedium — reduces warehousing costs; prevents write-offs
3. No Real-Time Stock ExposureMEDIUM

Production Manager receives 10–15 phone calls/day from Accounts verifying stock levels. This interrupts production management and wastes time on both sides.

MetricDetail
Time Waste10–15 min × 10–15 calls/day = 2–3 hrs/day of PM's time
InvestmentLow — Zoho Inventory live visibility for Accounts
ROIVery High — PM time recovered for production management
4. No Grade-Based Production CostingMEDIUM

Grades A, B, and C have different processing costs but are not costed separately. This contributes directly to the COGS volatility reported by Finance (50–77% margin swings).

MetricDetail
InvestmentMedium — costing methodology redesign with Finance
ROIHigh — accurate product-level profitability; better pricing
Cross-DepartmentFinance & Accounts
Section 04

Information Gaps & Cross-Department Leaks

⚠

Production ↔ Accounts (Stock Availability)

No live stock data available to Accounts. Every invoice requires a manual phone call. This is the most frequently reported cross-department friction across the organization.

⚠

Production ↔ Finance (WMS ↔ Books)

Custom Zoho WMS operates independently. Production costs, WIP data, and stock movements do not auto-flow to Zoho Books.

⚠

Production ↔ R&D (Lab-to-Commercial Handoff)

Formulation handoffs from R&D to Production are project-based and ad-hoc. No standardized handoff protocol exists for scaling lab formulations to commercial production.

Section 06

Proposed Solutions

Solution: WMS ↔ Zoho Books API Integration

Build API connection between the in-development Zoho WMS and Zoho Books. This is cheapest to fix now during development rather than retrofitting later.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1MediumAutomated cost flow; eliminates manual reconciliation; accurate COGS

Solution: Zoho Inventory Real-Time Exposure

Enable live Zoho Inventory visibility for Accounts team. Eliminates 10–15 daily phone calls to Production Manager.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P1LowRecovers 2–3 hrs/day of PM's time; eliminates interruptions

Solution: Grade-Based Costing Methodology

Work with Finance to implement grade-based (A/B/C) production costing with WIP valuation at each stage. Configure in Zoho Inventory.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P2MediumAccurate product-level profitability; resolves COGS volatility

Solution: Packaging MOQ Optimization

Explore: (1) Consortium buying with other brands to aggregate MOQs, (2) Digital printing suppliers with lower MOQs, (3) Standardized packaging with variable labels to reduce unique SKU count.

PriorityEffortExpected Impact
P3MediumReduces 68% surplus; lowers warehousing costs
