Section 01 — Executive Summary

Vertical Overview — B2B & RCM

Scope, current state, and headline findings

The B2B & RCM vertical manages major corporate accounts and white-label agreements, anchored by RCM. Bulk raw material is supplied to a central warehouse, which RCM distributes down to ~10,000 retail stores. A secondary broker channel (Seagreen) is also managed, where EFP uniquely bears the cost of tertiary liquidation.

The audit identified three critical structural failures: a complete lack of tertiary sales visibility below the RCM depot tier causing inventory blind spots, paper-based field data capture causing transcription delays and Accounts bottlenecks, and a 90% reliance on personal WhatsApp numbers for business comms, risking total data loss upon employee turnover. The vertical scores 1.3 maturity against a target of 3.0.

Section 03 — Process Detail

Current Workflows

Where each finding sits inside the operational process

Primary Supply & Tertiary Blind Spot — P-01

What is good: The primary B2B fulfillment sequence to the central RCM warehouse is well-defined and maintains a strict 20 MT standing inventory commitment.

What needs to be fixed: EFP has zero data visibility below the RCM regional depot tier. There is no automated feed from RCM's ERP to indicate actual off-take at the ~10,000 retail stores.

Bottlenecks: Because EFP relies on reactive 40kg depot threshold alerts rather than predictive point-of-sale data, Production and Logistics are forced into rapid, ad-hoc fulfillment cycles.

Dependencies: Production requires this tertiary data to plan manufacturing; Logistics requires it to schedule freight without rush orders.

Field Training Data & Transcription Cycle — P-02 P-03

What is good: Dedicated field team members actively capture ground-level training metrics and farmer engagement data.

What needs to be fixed: Data is captured in physical paper diaries and requires travel back to the office before manual transcription by Anil. This creates high error risk and delays Accounts reimbursement processing.

Bottlenecks: Manual transcription consumes back-office bandwidth and delays real-time visibility into field training impact.

Dependencies: Accounts relies on this transcribed data to process field staff expense reimbursements. Any delay in the diary handoff stalls payments.

Community Engagement Funnel & Drop-off — P-04

What is good: The team has successfully built a top-of-funnel community of 2,000 farmer contacts via WhatsApp, demonstrating strong initial outreach capability.

What needs to be fixed: The funnel suffers a 90% drop-off rate. No structured retargeting, FAQ automation, or QC feedback loop exists to re-engage the 1,800 inactive contacts.

Bottlenecks: Manual broadcasting via personal WhatsApp limits scale and provides zero engagement analytics.

Dependencies: Relies entirely on Piyush and the field team manually monitoring group activity, pulling time away from new training events.

Section 06 — Corrective Action Plan

Recommended Solution Segment

This section outlines the integrated technology solution proposed to resolve operational bottlenecks, replacing the legacy recommendations register. Built on the Solution Design Framework — extend existing tools, secure data on existing channels, integrate cross-departmentally.

Implementation Required

Solution Modules to be Built:

1
Supply Chain Visibility Integration (Cross-Dept)
  • RCM ERP API Bridge: Establish an automated data feed from RCM's ERP capturing tertiary store off-take. This data is piped directly into a live dashboard shared with Production and Logistics.
  • Predictive Threshold Replacing: Replaces the reactive "40kg depot alert" with actual point-of-sale trends, allowing Production to plan manufacturing cycles proactively for the 20 MT commitment.
  • Seagreen Liquidation Sync: Structured tracking for the broker channel to monitor EFP's true tertiary liquidation costs and dispatch statuses in real-time.
2
Digital Field Operations & Data Security (Extend)
  • Offline Mobile Forms: Replace physical paper diaries with an offline-capable mobile form (e.g., Zoho/Google Forms). Field team logs training data on the ground; it auto-syncs to Google Sheets when online. Zero transcription needed.
  • WhatsApp Business API Migration: Transition the 90% of personal WhatsApp comms to company-owned Virtual Business Lines. Secures all farmer data, groups, and chat histories against employee turnover.
3
AI WhatsApp Community Bot (Custom Build)
  • Automated FAQ & Retargeting: Deploy a custom Gemini-powered WhatsApp bot to handle community FAQs, run automated QC feedback polls, and systematically retarget the 90% (1,800) inactive contacts.
  • Auto-Logging: Bot interactions automatically log farmer engagement data back into the Google Sheet, providing Piyush and Jatin with real-time community health metrics without manual monitoring.

What Remains the Same

  • No New CRM: Google Sheets remains the system of record for field data. Forms simply push data directly into it.
  • Familiar Channel: The team and farmers continue using WhatsApp. The transition is backend (Personal → Business API), invisible to the farmer.

Tech Stack Utilized

  • Frontend: ReactJS + TypeScript (Dashboard)
  • APIs: RCM ERP, WhatsApp Business, Google Sheets, Zoho Forms
  • AI: Gemini LLM (WhatsApp Bot)
  • Host: Google Cloud Platform

Key Operational Problems Solved

  • Eliminates the tertiary sales blind spot, stabilizing the 20 MT inventory flow for Production & Logistics.
  • Removes 20% of manual back-office transcription time via direct-to-sheet mobile forms.
  • Secures 100% of business comms and farmer data against employee turnover.
  • Re-engages 1,800 inactive community contacts through automated WhatsApp retargeting.

Future Scope of Improvement / Enhancement

  • Predictive Analytics Engine: Once tertiary RCM data is flowing, an AI layer could be added to predict seasonal demand spikes across the 10,000 stores, further optimizing Production planning. Not needed in Phase 1 but a planned future enhancement.

Next Steps for Team Adoption

  • Mandatory Training: Field team must complete walkthrough sessions on the new mobile forms app before Phase 2 rollout.
  • Device Policy: Formalize the transition to Virtual Business Lines; personal numbers will be phased out for business comms.
  • Feedback Loop: Establish a continuous feedback loop with Anil (Back Office) to ensure the auto-synced Sheets match the previous manual format.
