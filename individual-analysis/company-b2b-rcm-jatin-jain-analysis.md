## 3. B2B Distribution Structure (RCM Specific)
RCM operates a three-tier distribution model that is independent of EFP:

```
[EFP Production / Coimbatore]
             | (Primary Dispatch)
   1. [RCM Central Warehouse] (Pan-India) -- EFP Transaction Point
             | (Secondary Dispatch)
   2. [Regional Depot / Stockist]
             |
   3. [Retail Stores] (~10,000 outlets pan-India)
```

* **Data Visibility Limits:** EFP has inventory visibility at the Central Warehouse and Regional Depot layers. EFP has **no visibility into store-level data** (stated directly).
* **Replenishment Threshold:** Depot-level inventory is tracked for demand planning. Reorders are triggered when local depot stocks drop below a **40 kg threshold** (stated directly).
* **Manufacturing Division:** EFP ships raw components (unformulated Components "A" and "B") from South India. Final formulation, mixing, and retail packaging are executed at RCM's factory in Bhilwara, Rajasthan.

## 4. Workflows & Lifecycle Processes

### Order Routing & Dispatch Workflow
When RCM or a white-label client issues a Purchase Order (PO):
1. **PO Receipt:** Jatin Jain receives client PO.
2. **Production Routing:** PO is routed to Production to confirm raw material availability.
3. **Accounts Invoicing:** Production logs are shared with the Accounts team for invoice generation.
4. **Logistics Booking:** Logistics coordinates carrier routing and dispatch schedules.
5. **Final Execution:** Logistics executes the shipment to the central warehouse or specified depot.

### White-Label Branding & Packaging Workflow
1. **Finalize Design:** The packaging layout is aligned with client specifications.
2. **Print Run:** finalized design is sent to third-party printers. Turnaround time for design-to-delivery is **10 days** (stated directly).
3. **Packaging Stocking:** Printed bags are delivered to the RCM facility in Bhilwara or EFP Coimbatore for final filling.

## 5. Tooling & Information Infrastructure
* **Data Recording:** Managed via ad-hoc, individual spreadsheets on Google Sheets. There is no central CRM or system of record.
* **Field Tracking:** Training events, attendee logs, and local contact numbers are manually documented in physical vendor diaries/registers. This data is physically collected and transcribed into Excel by back-office staff.
* **Communications:** Managed via Slack (internal), WhatsApp (Middle East), WeChat (China), and LINE (Japan).
* **Expense Management:** Zoho Expense is used to manage employee travel claims. Travel expenses are paid out-of-pocket by employees, who submit monthly claims via Zoho. Reimbursement policy requires a three-level approval chain (Executor → Manager → Senior Admin).
