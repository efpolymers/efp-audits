## **5\. Scope of Work and Execution Plan**

The following modules have been engineered to upgrade the platform. Execution will be handled via a continuous rollout cycle.

**Module 1: Role-Based Multi-User Access Control (RBAC)**

* **Features:** Structural database overhaul, department-specific workspaces (Operations, Sales, PRO, CXO), and granular permission gating.  
* **User Journey:** An employee logs into the platform, bypassing the general admin view. They land on a tailored dashboard showing only the tasks, client data, and permissions relevant to their specific department, ensuring absolute data security and scalable team structure.  
* **Timeline:** 1 Week

**Module 2: WhatsApp-Centric Automation**

* **Features:** Automated task reminders, Sales Enquiry, Lead Qualification, Lead Meeting booking using natural language conversation, and direct document re-uploads via API integration  
* **User Journey:** A client receives an automated WhatsApp message reminding them of a missing signature. The client replies directly with a photo of the signed document in the chat. The system automatically routes that file into their core profile on the platform without requiring the client to log into a web link.  
  A new lead applies for a new company registration on a website, gets a whatsapp message to book a call with our expert team member to give them a walkthrough of their Business Setup Journey. Post that whatsapp can send their automated updates and reminders for any necessary steps.  
* **Timeline:** 1–2 Weeks

**Module 3: Smart Document Intelligence System**

* **Features:** Pre-upload image sanitization, automated OCR data extraction, expiry date tracking, and multi-page mismatch detection.  
* **User Journey:** A client uploads their trade license. Instead of waiting for a human to review it, the system instantly scans the document. If the image is blurry, it flags the user to retake the photo immediately. If clear, the Node.js backend extracts the company name and expiry date, automatically populating the database.  
* **Timeline:** 3–4 Weeks

**Module 4: Internal Task Allocation & Assignment Engine**

* **Features:** Multi-tier task assignment, internal workload visibility boards, and automated SLA deadline tracking.  
* **User Journey:** An operations manager receives a new lead and assigns it to a specific PRO team member. The assignee receives a real-time notification. Once the assignee completes the task, the reviewer is automatically pinged to provide final approval, leaving a clear digital audit trail.  
* **Timeline:** 2–3 Weeks

**Module 5: AI Operations Copilot for Management Team**

* **Features:** Case status synthesizer, automated email drafting, and early threat warning detection for stalled pipelines.  
* **User Journey:** A Director wants an update on a complex, multi-week client onboarding. Instead of reading through 50 lines of logs, they ask the Copilot. The AI instantly generates a 3-bullet summary of the current bottlenecks and drafts an update email to send to the client.  
* **Timeline:** 3–5 Weeks

**Module 6: Escalation & Red Flag Monitoring System**

* **Features:** SLA breach alerts, internal inactivity protocols, and visual dashboard indicators for at-risk cases.  
* **User Journey:** If an internal staff member fails to act on a client document for 48 hours, the system highlights the case in red on the management dashboard and sends an automated WhatsApp escalation alert to the department head.  
* **Timeline:** 1–2 Weeks

**Module 7: Operational Analytics Dashboard**

* **Features:** Active pipeline tracking, staff throughput metrics, and document rejection pattern analysis.  
* **User Journey:** Leadership opens the analytics interface to view real-time graphs showing average processing times across different departments, instantly identifying which external partners are driving the fastest conversions.  
* **Timeline:** 2–3 Weeks


**Total Transformation Time: 13-16 Weeks**
