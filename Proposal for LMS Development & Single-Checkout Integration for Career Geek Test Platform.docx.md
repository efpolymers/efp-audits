## **1\. Overview & Executive Summary**

**Project:** Learning Management System (LMS) Integration & Unified Checkout Setup for Careergeek.

**Purpose:** To expand Careergeek’s live Test Series platform by building a complete Learning Management System directly inside their current test platform. Instead of setting up a separate software tool that forces students to visit a different web address, this new video learning module will live right next to their existing practice tests.

**Outcome:** An all-in-one preparation platform for GMAT, GRE, and SAT students. We are setting up a "Single Checkout Pass", meaning a student makes one purchase, and the system automatically unlocks both their video lessons and their practice tests / full length tests at the same time. Your team will manage everything from your current admin dashboard, allowing you to add users, monitor login security, and control who sees what, all from one place.

## 

## **2\. Business Opportunity and Problem Statement**

**The Challenge:** When educational businesses expand from practice tests into video lectures, they often bolt on a second, separate software platform. This creates an immediate operational headache: students have to remember two different logins, buying a bundle requires complicated discount codes or double checkouts, and your team is forced to update student access across two separate spreadsheets or databases.

**The Solution:** We are building this Learning Management System directly on top of what you already have. By expanding your existing test platform, students get a clean, organized video classroom right beside their test suites. We will also upgrade your payment system so that the exact second a payment clears, the website automatically checks two boxes behind the scenes, giving the student instant access to both their videos and their exams, while keeping all administration inside your existing control panel.

## **5\. Detailed Scope of Work (Phased Execution Plan)**

The following modules have been engineered to upgrade the platform. Execution will be handled via a continuous rollout cycle.

### **Phase 0: Existing Platform Audit (1-2 Days)**

A critical first step is to review your current live platform and ensure that our new building work does not disrupt any students who are currently taking practice tests.

* **System Checkup:** We will review your current database and platform to map out the exact spots where the new video features will attach.  
* **Safe Testing Environment:** We will create an isolated staging environment of the current platform (if not already present) . All coding and testing will happen here first so your live platform remains 100% stable and untouched.

### **Phase 1: LMS on user side Interface (1-3 Weeks)**

* **Organized Course Navigation:** Building a clean, menu based system that organizes your content logically.  
* **Distraction-Free Video Player:** Adding a smooth video player with speed controls, quality settings, a "Mark as Completed" button, and an interactive study notepad sitting right next to the screen so students can type notes while watching.  
* **4 to 5 Device Security Limit:** Building intelligent security rules that remember the devices a student uses to log in. If an account tries to watch videos from a 6th device, the system automatically blocks the stream or logs out the oldest device to prevent unauthorized password sharing.

### **Phase 2: Single-Payment Checkout Setup (1 Week)**

* **Bundle Checkout Setup:** Updating your checkout page so you can sell combined packages (e.g., "GMAT Master Bundle \= Video Course \+ Practice Tests").  
* **Instant Unlock Automation:** Upgrading the background Webhook with your payment gateway (Razorpay). The system automatically updates their account to unlock both the video library and the test series.

### **Phase 3: Admin Dashboard Upgrade (1-2 Weeks)**

* **Adding Switches to Your Existing Admin Panel:** We will add new control switches directly onto the student management screens your team already uses every day.  
* **Easy Staff Controls:** Giving your team simple buttons to manually enroll students who paid offline, extend course expiry dates, reset a student's device limit if they bought a new laptop, or turn off access with a single click.

### **Phase 4: Thorough Testing & Smooth Launch (1 Week)**

* **End-to-End Testing:** Our team will run thorough tests across the buying process, device limits, and video streaming to ensure everything runs without glitches.  
* **Go-Live:** We will safely launch the new features to your live website during a scheduled, low-traffic overnight window to ensure zero disruption to active users.

**Total Transformation Time: 6-7 Weeks**

## **6\. Video Hosting Comparison & Advice**

To help you choose the best way to store and stream your proprietary video lessons (GMAT, GRE, SAT), we have compared the two most practical options:

| Comparison Factor | Option A: Secure Private Cloud (AWS Cloud \+ Custom Video Streaming) | Option B: YouTube Embedding (Unlisted / Private Videos) |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Security & Video Protection | High (Professional Grade). Your video files are scrambled into special, un-downloadable fragments. They can only play inside your LMS. Downloader plugins and browser extensions cannot steal or save your videos. | Low (Vulnerable to Leaks). While unlisted videos do not show up in YouTube searches, anyone can inspect the video, copy the underlying YouTube link, and share it with non-paying students across the internet. |
| Ongoing Hosting Cost | You pay based on actual student data usage (\~₹3,000 to ₹6,500 / month for a starting group of 500 active students) Depending upon streaming minutes. | Zero Hosting Cost. Google covers 100% of the video storage and bandwidth costs free of charge. |
| Streaming Quality & Speed | High. Videos load instantly through a global delivery network that automatically adjusts video quality (1080p down to 480p) depending on how fast the student's internet is. | High. Leverages YouTube’s standard global video delivery speed. |
| Daily Work for Your Staff | Low (Automated). Your staff simply drops video files into your Careergeek admin panel; our system automatically handles the uploading, scrambling, and organizing behind the scenes. | High (Manual). Your team has to upload videos to YouTube, set privacy settings, create playlists, and manually copy-paste individual web links into the admin panel for every single lesson. |
| Our Recommendation | **Recommended for Careergeek.** Because your business revenue relies on the exclusivity of your paid courses, protecting your videos from being stolen or shared is easily worth the small monthly cloud storage cost. | **Not Recommended for Paid Courses.** Best suited only for free promotional videos or basic tutorials where someone copying the link will not hurt your revenue. |

## **20\. Risks and Mitigation Strategies**

* **Risk:** Adding new data rules could accidentally disturb student records on the active, live testing platform.  
  * *Our Solution:* We follow a strict "additive-only" rule. New video settings are added as optional, background additions to existing user profiles. All work is built and thoroughly tested on an isolated copy of your website first before ever touching the live system.  
* **Risk:** Payment gateway communication delays causing a customer to buy a bundle but wait several minutes for their videos to unlock.  
  * *Our Solution:* We build a dual-verification system. If the bank's automatic payment confirmation is delayed by internet traffic, the website independently checks the payment status the moment the student lands on their dashboard, ensuring instant unlocking.
